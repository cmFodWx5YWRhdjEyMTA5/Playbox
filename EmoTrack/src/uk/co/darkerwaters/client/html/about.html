About

Hello, I am glad you are interested and have come across my site. As you may be able to tell this is not a professional money-making web-site, just something I have created while ill myself. Two years prior to creating this site, I was diagnosed with Chronic-Fatigue, in my efforts to stabilise my symptoms and get back to a more active social and work life I looked a lot for something to track my mental state, and its effects on my fatigue. I found very little, and since my day-job is the creation of software, I thought "why not create my own?". So here it is.

Goals

I have no plans to put too much into the site as it makes me nothing and I intend to keep it that way. If, however, you experience any problems or have a need for something new please use the "contact" page to let me know what you would like. You never know, I might just add it.

Site Technology

The site itself is created using Google's GWT technology and the FlatUI styling library. This choice was made quite simply because I was interested in the technologies and it was a good opportunity to investigate and practice them. I found GWT in particular to be very good at creating on-line applications and the combination with the FlatUI javascript and css taught me to interface with the wider world of open-source javascript resources. In the future, I think I will be using more GWT in combination with various things I can find for free on the Internet to style it nicer.

As it is created predominantly with Google technologies, I suspect that it works best with Google's own Chrome browser but it does promise to work with them all and I have tested the application in everything I sensibly can.

If you are wondering how I track my activity, sleep, and deep sleep, then I suggest you check out the Misfit Range of fitness trackers. I found the Shine to be a nice simple device that allows me to track activity, sleep, and deep sleep without constantly charging the thing.
