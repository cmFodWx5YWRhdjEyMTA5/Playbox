CBT Emotion Tracking

Tracking your emotions lies at the heart of CBT (Cognitive Behavioural Therapy) more . As part of treatment for all kinds of problems, including but not limited to; depression, anxiety, chronic fatigue, it is a good idea to regularly track how you are feeling so when you look back on your week / month / year, you can properly see how you felt. Hopefully seeing that it was not as bad as you thought, and you are better over time.
